Research & Innovation

Research Groups
at ChE BUET

The Department conducts research spanning a wide range of chemical engineering subfields — process systems engineering, safety engineering, biochemical engineering & biotechnology, environmental engineering, and fuel & energy systems. Our research groups undertake exciting and impactful projects in collaboration with national and international experts.

Our Groups

Active Research Groups

🔥

BUET CPI Safety Research Group

Process Safety · Chemical Process Industries

Focused on hazard identification, risk analysis, and safety management in chemical process industries. Research covers fire safety, explosion risk, industrial disaster prevention, and the development of safety standards relevant to Bangladeshi industry.

Key Faculty

Dr. Syeda Sultana Razia Dr. Md. Easir Arafat Khan
Visit Group Website →
⚙️

Process Systems Engineering Research Group

Process Control · Modelling · Optimisation

Advancing the design, control, and optimisation of chemical processes through mathematical modelling, simulation, and data-driven methods. Research topics include controller performance monitoring, process identification, and plantwide control.

Key Faculty

Dr. Md. Ali Ahammad Shoukat Choudhury Dr. Nahid Sanzida
Visit Group Website →
🌿

Sustainability in Energy & Environment Research (SEER)

Energy · Environment · Sustainability

Investigating sustainable pathways for energy production and environmental remediation. Research spans solid fuels, gasification, biomass-to-liquid conversion, CO₂ utilisation, low-carbon technologies, and the hydrogen economy.

Key Faculty

Dr. Kazi Bayzid Kabir Dr. Kawnish Kirtania
Visit Group Website →
🧬

Quantitative & Environmental Biotechnology Group (QEB)

Bioenvironmental · Bioprocess · Medical Imaging

Applying quantitative methods to biological and environmental systems. Research covers bioenvironmental engineering, biological and medical image analysis, quantitative biology and biotechnology, biochemical & bioprocess engineering, and air pollution modelling.

Key Faculty

Dr. Shoeb Ahmed Dr. Nafisa Islam
Visit Group Website →
🧫

Bio & Environmental Technology Group

Biotechnology · Environmental Engineering · Sustainability

Integrating biotechnology and environmental engineering to develop sustainable industrial solutions. Research interests include biosensing, bio-surface engineering, paper-based diagnostics, biomaterials, and environmental remediation technologies.

Key Faculty

Dr. Mohidus Samad Khan
Visit Group Website →
🌍

Environment & Sustainability Research Group (ESRG)

Environmental Science · Nanocomposites · Water Treatment

Pursuing research at the interface of environmental science and chemical engineering. Focus areas include adsorption, biochar synthesis, advanced oxidation processes, nanocomposite synthesis, physico-chemical and biological water treatment.

Key Faculty

Dr. Md. Shahinoor Islam Dr. Iftheker Ahmed Khan
Visit Group Website →
