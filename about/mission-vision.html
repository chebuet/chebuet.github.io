Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles and long-term aspirations

Our Purpose

Mission Statement

M

The mission of the Department of Chemical Engineering at BUET is to provide an exceptional educational environment that equips students with a strong foundation in chemical engineering principles, fosters innovative research, and cultivates professional and ethical engineers who contribute meaningfully to Bangladesh's industrial development and global engineering challenges.

🏫
Education

Deliver high-quality undergraduate and graduate education in chemical engineering

🔬
Research

Advance knowledge through innovative research addressing national and global needs

🤝
Service

Engage with industry and society to translate knowledge into practical solutions

Our Aspiration

Vision Statement

To be a world-class department of chemical engineering, recognized for academic excellence, impactful research, and graduates who drive technological innovation and sustainable industrial growth in Bangladesh and beyond.

Goals

Program Educational Objectives

Graduates of our programs are expected to demonstrate the following outcomes within several years of graduation:

  1. Technical Competence: Apply scientific and engineering principles to solve complex chemical engineering problems.
  2. Professional Practice: Work effectively in multidisciplinary teams and communicate results clearly in written and oral forms.
  3. Lifelong Learning: Engage in continuous professional development and stay current with emerging technologies.
  4. Ethics & Society: Demonstrate ethical responsibility and commitment to sustainable engineering practice.
  5. Leadership: Advance to positions of leadership and influence in industry, government, or academia.
