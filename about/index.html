About the Department

Chemical Engineering at BUET — Over Six Decades of Excellence

Overview

Who We Are

The Department of Chemical Engineering at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) is one of the pioneering engineering departments in Bangladesh, established in 1961. Over more than six decades, the department has been a cornerstone of engineering education and research in the country, producing graduates who have gone on to distinguished careers in industry, government, and academia across the world.

The department offers rigorous undergraduate (B.Sc. in Chemical Engineering), postgraduate (M.Sc. Engg.), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs. With a strong emphasis on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, the curriculum is regularly updated to reflect global developments in the field.

Research in the department spans a diverse range of areas including process systems engineering, environmental engineering, energy and fuels, biochemical engineering, and materials & nanotechnology. The department is home to state-of-the-art laboratories and computing facilities that support cutting-edge research.
