Joining the Programme
Admission Information

Applications are accepted twice a year — January and July sessions. The process is competitive and merit-based.

Eligibility — MSc

  • BSc in Chemical Engineering or closely related discipline from a recognised university
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.5 out of 4.0 (or equivalent) in undergraduate studies
  • Written admission test: Mathematics, Chemical Engineering fundamentals
  • Viva-voce examination by department committee

Eligibility — PhD

  • MSc in Chemical Engineering or equivalent from a recognised institution
  • Minimum CGPA of 3.0 out of 4.0 in MSc or BSc with strong research record
  • Statement of research intent and proposed topic
  • Prior identification of a willing faculty supervisor is strongly recommended
  • Approval from BUET Postgraduate Committee

Supervisor Selection

All MSc and PhD students must identify and confirm a faculty supervisor within the first semester. Selection is subject to faculty availability and research alignment.

Supervisor Selection Portal

Submit your supervisor preference through the official online portal. New students should consult with the postgraduate coordinator before submitting. Deadline for July 2025 intake: 31 August 2025.

Open Portal →

Supervisor Selection Guidelines

  • Review research areas of all faculty members in the Faculty section below before submitting
  • You may list up to 3 preferences in order of priority; the committee will try to accommodate the first choice
  • Contact prospective supervisors by email to discuss research compatibility before the portal deadline
  • PhD students must include a 1-page research proposal aligned with the supervisor's area
  • Final allocation is done by the Postgraduate Committee and is binding
  • Supervisor changes after allocation are exceptional and require written justification
