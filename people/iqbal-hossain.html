Research Interests

Fuel Chemistry & Technology Energy Management Cleaner Production Separation Processes Catalysis & Kinetics Multiphase Flow Hydrodynamics Polymer & PVC Technology Food Colorants & Safety

Dr. Hossain's research spans fuel chemistry, cleaner production, and separation engineering. He received his doctorate from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, one of Asia's premier research institutions. His work addresses both fundamental science — catalysis, kinetics, and hydrodynamics in multiphase flow — and applied challenges including energy management and polymer/PVC technology relevant to Bangladesh's industrial landscape. He also serves on the editorial board of the Chemical Engineering Research Bulletin.
