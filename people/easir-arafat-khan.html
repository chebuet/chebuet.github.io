Research Interests

Chemical Safety & Security Process Safety Fire Safety & Risk Quantitative Risk Analysis Hazardous Materials Transport Adsorption & Catalysis Zeolite Membranes Environmental Remediation

Dr. Easir Arafat Khan is a leading authority on chemical and process safety in Bangladesh. He completed his doctorate at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore (2011) and undertook a visiting scholarship at the Mary Kay O'Connor Process Safety Center, Texas A&M University (2016). He is currently the Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Chemical Engineering (IEB) and Chief Scientific Advisor to the Centre for Climate Justice, Bangladesh. His technical investigations on industrial fire incidents — including the high-profile 2024 Baily Road restaurant fire — have established him as a trusted public expert on fire safety.
