Research Interests

Process Control & Performance Assessment Control Valve Stiction Data Science & Mining Signal Processing Condition-Based Monitoring Plant-Wide Oscillation Diagnosis Fault Detection & Diagnosis Energy Modelling & Auditing Air & Water Pollution

Prof. Choudhury is an internationally recognised authority in process systems engineering with a particular focus on diagnosing and remedying poor control performance in large industrial plants. His data-driven methods for detecting control valve stiction have been implemented in commercial software (ProcessDoctor, Matrikon Inc.) and applied extensively in chemical, petrochemical, oil & gas, mineral processing, and pulp & paper industries worldwide. He is the principal inventor of an international patent on Methods for Detection and Quantification of Control Valve Stiction and the lead author of the seminal Springer-Verlag monograph on the topic (2008).
