Research Interests

H₂ Energy & Green Hydrogen Bioenergy & Biofuels Thermochemical Conversion Hydrothermal Carbonization Pyrolysis & Gasification CO₂ Utilization Waste Valorization Techno-Economic Analysis Life Cycle Assessment Process Modelling

Dr. Kirtania is the Principal Investigator of the ReHyCaRe (Renewable Hydrogen Generation with Carbon Recycling) project, which converts biogenic agricultural residues of Bangladesh into clean hydrogen fuel. He received his doctorate from Monash University, Australia (2014) and undertook post-doctoral research at Luleå University of Technology, Sweden (2015–2017), working on pilot-scale catalytic biomass gasification. His group has published more than 40 peer-reviewed journal articles, 2 book chapters (Elsevier), and over 30 conference contributions, and he serves as reviewer for 17 international journals. Dr. Kirtania was awarded the prestigious NASEM/USAID PEER Research Grant.
