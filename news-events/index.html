Research June 15, 2025

ChE Researchers Publish Breakthrough in Biodiesel Production from Local Feedstock

A research team led by Dr. Md. Rahman has developed a novel catalytic process that increases biodiesel yield by 34% using locally available Jatropha feedstock. The findings have been published in Fuel Processing Technology.
