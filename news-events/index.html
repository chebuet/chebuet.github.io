📅 May 16, 2025  ·  Published in Science

Collaborative Work from BUET Published in Prestigious Science Journal

A collaborative study by Professor Shoeb Ahmed of the Department of Chemical Engineering, BUET — alongside researchers from Stanford University, Boston University, icddr,b, and Greentech Knowledge Solutions — has been published in Science, one of the world's most prestigious scientific journals. The study introduces an evidence-based strategy proven to increase energy efficiency and reduce emissions in Bangladesh's brick manufacturing industry. Their work featured as the cover of the issue, demonstrating its global significance.

Cover Story

📅 May 16, 2025  ·  Science Journal

Collaborative Work from BUET Published in Science — Brick Kiln Energy Efficiency

Prof. Shoeb Ahmed (ChE, BUET) and an international team from Stanford, Boston University, icddr,b, and Greentech Knowledge Solutions published a randomised controlled trial (RCT) in Science demonstrating that brick kiln owners in Bangladesh voluntarily adopt cleaner, more efficient practices when given proper training and profit-aligned support. The study covered 276 kilns in the 2022–2023 brick kiln season and featured as the cover of its issue.
