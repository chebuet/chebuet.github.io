History, vision, and mission of Chemical Engineering at BUET.

Overview

The Department of Chemical Engineering at BUET is one of the leading institutions in Bangladesh, offering rigorous academic programs and impactful research in energy, environment, and process engineering.

Mission & Vision

Mission
To educate engineers and conduct research addressing global challenges.
Vision
To be a global leader in chemical engineering education and innovation.
Values
Excellence, integrity, sustainability, and collaboration.
