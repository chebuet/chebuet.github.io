About the Department

Excellence in chemical engineering education, research, and innovation since 1972

Who We Are

Mission & Vision

Mission: To produce world-class chemical engineers who can address national and global challenges in energy, environment, materials, and process industries through innovative research and ethical practice.

Vision: To become a leading center of excellence in chemical engineering education and research in South Asia, recognized globally for impactful contributions to sustainable development.

The Department of Chemical Engineering at BUET was established in 1972. Since then, we have produced over 3,000 graduates who now serve as leaders in academia, industry, government, and entrepreneurship across Bangladesh and around the world.

Our Journey

History

1972
Department Founded — Chemical Engineering established as a separate department at BUET, initially offering only undergraduate programs.
1985
First M.Sc. Program — Graduate program launched to meet growing demand for advanced education in chemical engineering.
2002
Ph.D. Program Introduced — Doctoral program established, enabling cutting-edge research in catalysis, environment, and energy.
2018
New Research Building — State-of-the-art research facilities inaugurated, including advanced characterization labs.
2025
Global Recognition — BUET ChemE ranked among top 300 chemical engineering departments worldwide by QS.
Our People

Department Community

Our department is a vibrant community of 28 full-time faculty members, over 600 undergraduate and graduate students, and 20+ administrative and technical staff. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive environment that encourages collaboration across disciplines.

Faculty members hold doctoral degrees from leading universities around the world, including MIT, Imperial College, University of Cambridge, University of Tokyo, and many others. Our students regularly win national and international awards for their research and innovation.
