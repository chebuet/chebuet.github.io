Academic Programs

🎓
4-Year Program

Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering

Our flagship undergraduate program provides a rigorous foundation in core chemical engineering disciplines — thermodynamics, transport phenomena, reaction engineering, and process design — complemented by strong laboratory training and industry exposure.

  • ✓ Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • ✓ Total Credits: 160
  • ✓ Intake: ~60 students per year
  • ✓ Admission: Through BUET Undergraduate Admission Test

Graduate Studies

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate offerings — M.Sc. Engineering (thesis & non-thesis) and Ph.D. — are designed for engineers seeking to deepen their expertise and contribute to original research across our core research areas.

  • ✓ M.Sc. Engineering (Thesis): 18–24 months
  • ✓ M.Sc. Engineering (Non-Thesis): 12–18 months
  • ✓ Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.): 3–5 years
  • ✓ Two intakes per year (January & July)

Curriculum

Core Academic Areas

⚗️

Chemical Thermodynamics

Phase equilibria, reaction equilibria, and energy balances forming the theoretical backbone of process engineering.

🌊

Transport Phenomena

Momentum, heat, and mass transfer — the governing principles behind every industrial separation and reaction system.

🔬

Reaction Engineering

Design and analysis of chemical reactors, kinetics, and catalysis for industrial-scale chemical production.

⚙️

Process Design & Control

Systematic methodology for designing, simulating, and controlling chemical processes safely and efficiently.

🌱

Environmental Engineering

Pollution prevention, waste treatment, and sustainability principles integrated throughout the curriculum.

💻

Computational Methods

Numerical methods, process simulation, and data-driven approaches for modern chemical engineering practice.
