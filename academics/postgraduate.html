Postgraduate Programs

M.Sc. Engg. and Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering

Graduate Studies

Programs at a Glance

🎓

M.Sc. Engg. (Thesis)

Research-focused two-year program requiring a thesis. Ideal for those pursuing academic or industrial research careers.

Duration2 Years
Credits36 (incl. thesis)
ModeFull-time / Part-time
📖

M.Sc. Engg. (Coursework)

Coursework-based program suitable for working professionals seeking advanced technical knowledge without thesis research.

Duration1.5–2 Years
Credits36 (coursework)
ModePart-time preferred
🔬

Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering

Doctoral program emphasising original research contributions in chemical engineering. Typically 3–5 years.

Duration3–5 Years
Credits18 + Dissertation
ModeFull-time

Admission

Admission Requirements

Eligibility for M.Sc. Engg.

  • B.Sc. Engg. (Chemical Engineering) from BUET or equivalent institution with at least 2nd Class or CGPA ≥ 2.75/4.00
  • Candidates from other engineering backgrounds may be considered with the approval of the departmental postgraduate committee
  • A valid GRE score is preferred for international applicants
  • Students must identify a willing supervisor before the application deadline

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Transcripts of all academic records
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Statement of Purpose (1,000 words max)
  • National ID or passport copy

Eligibility for Ph.D.

  • M.Sc. Engg. or equivalent with CGPA ≥ 3.00/4.00 from a recognized university
  • Exceptional B.Sc. graduates (CGPA ≥ 3.75) may be considered for direct Ph.D. entry
  • Research proposal (2,000 words) submitted to the prospective supervisor prior to application
  • Departmental screening interview may be required

Funding Opportunities

Ph.D. students may be eligible for BUET fellowships, research assistantships from faculty grants, government scholarships (NST, BANBEIS), and international collaborations.
