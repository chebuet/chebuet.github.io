Research

Research Groups

Our research groups bring together faculty and students to tackle focused problems across chemical engineering's frontiers. Click on any group to learn more.

⚙️
PSE

Process Systems Engineering

The PSE group focuses on the mathematical modelling, simulation, optimisation, and control of complex chemical and biochemical processes. Key areas include process integration, energy efficiency, and systems-level approaches to sustainable manufacturing.

Process Optimisation Simulation Control Systems Energy Integration
Learn More →
🌱
SEER

Sustainability in Engineering & Environment Research

SEER addresses the critical intersection of engineering and environmental sustainability. The group conducts research in green chemistry, life-cycle assessment, carbon capture, renewable energy integration, and sustainable industrial practices tailored to Bangladesh's development needs.

Green Chemistry Life-Cycle Assessment Carbon Capture Renewable Energy
Learn More →
🧫
BET

Bio and Environmental Technology

The BET group bridges biological sciences and chemical engineering to develop innovative solutions for environmental remediation, biofuel production, wastewater treatment, and biosensor development. The group's work is closely aligned with Bangladesh's challenges in water quality and agro-industrial waste valorisation.

Biofuels Wastewater Treatment Biosensors Bioremediation
Learn More →
🔥
RCE

Reaction & Catalysis Engineering

Focused on heterogeneous catalysis, reactor design, and reaction kinetics, this group works on developing novel catalysts and processes for petroleum refining, polymer synthesis, and emerging green chemistry routes relevant to Bangladesh's industry.

Heterogeneous Catalysis Reactor Design Kinetics Polymer Synthesis
Learn More →
🧲
MNT

Materials & Nanotechnology

The MNT group investigates the synthesis, characterisation, and application of advanced materials including nanomaterials, membranes, composites, and smart materials. Applications span energy storage, drug delivery, water purification, and functional coatings.

Nanomaterials Membranes Energy Storage Drug Delivery Composites

Infrastructure

Research Facilities

🏛️

Unit Operations Lab

Equipped with pilot-scale distillation, absorption, and extraction units for experimental research and teaching.

⚗️

Reaction Engineering Lab

Houses batch and continuous reactors, along with in-situ analytical instruments for kinetic studies.

🔭

Materials Characterisation Lab

Instruments for surface area analysis, electron microscopy, XRD, FTIR, and thermal analysis.

💧

Environmental Analysis Lab

Water quality monitoring, pollutant analysis, and environmental impact assessment capabilities.

🧬

Bioprocess Lab

Bioreactors, fermenters, and downstream processing equipment for biotechnology research.

💻

Computational Lab

High-performance computing nodes with commercial (Aspen, HYSYS) and open-source simulation software.
