B.Sc. in Chemical Engineering — Four-Year Honours Degree

Program

Program Overview

The Department of Chemical Engineering at BUET offers a four-year Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree in Chemical Engineering. Established in 1961, this program is one of the most prestigious in Bangladesh, combining rigorous theoretical foundations with hands-on laboratory and design experience.

Graduates are equipped to work in the chemical, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, food processing, polymer, energy, and environmental sectors, or to pursue advanced studies and research. The curriculum is continuously updated to reflect the evolving needs of industry and society.

Admission

Admission Requirements

Admission to the B.Sc. Chemical Engineering program is through the BUET Undergraduate Admission Test, conducted annually. Candidates must fulfil the following minimum requirements:

  • Minimum GPA of 5.00 in both SSC and HSC examinations (Science Group)
  • Pass in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry at HSC level
  • Bangladeshi citizenship or NRB status
  • Qualify in the BUET Admission Test (held every academic year)
