Overview

About BRTC — Chemical Engineering

The Bureau of Research, Testing and Consultation (BRTC) is BUET's institutional mechanism for delivering professional technical services to public and private sector organizations. Operating under the central BRTC of BUET, the Chemical Engineering Department's BRTC cell offers specialized testing and consultancy in areas aligned with its research and academic strengths.

Our services support industries in food processing, pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, water treatment, textile, cement, and other chemical process sectors. Tests are conducted under the supervision of qualified faculty using calibrated, modern laboratory equipment.

Clients requiring testing or consultation are requested to contact the departmental BRTC coordinator or the central BRTC office to initiate a request. A formal work order and quotation will be issued after review.

Process

How to Request a Service

Contact the Office
Reach out via email or phone to the departmental BRTC coordinator or the central BRTC office to discuss your requirement.
Submit Application
Submit a formal application letter on your organization's letterhead describing the test(s) or service required.
Work Order & Payment
A work order and fee challan will be issued. Payment is made to the designated BUET account before work commences.
Receive Report
An official test report on BRTC letterhead is issued upon completion and can be collected from the departmental office.
