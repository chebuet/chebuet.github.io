Services

Bureau of Research, Testing & Consultation

The Bureau of Research, Testing and Consultation (BRTC) of the Department of Chemical Engineering, BUET, provides testing, analysis, and consultancy services to industry, government agencies, and research institutions. Operating since the department's early years, BRTC leverages the department's laboratories and expert faculty to deliver reliable, accredited results.

What We Offer

Services

🧪

Chemical Analysis

Quantitative and qualitative analysis of chemical samples, water, effluents, fuels, and industrial materials.

🔬

Material Testing

Corrosion testing, material characterisation, polymer analysis, and mechanical property assessment.

🛢️

Fuel & Petroleum

Characterisation and testing of fuels, lubricants, petroleum products, and related industrial materials.

🌿

Environmental Testing

Water quality assessment, effluent analysis, air quality monitoring, and environmental impact studies.

💡

Technical Consultancy

Expert consultancy on process design, safety, environmental compliance, and industrial problem-solving.

📋

Research Support

Support for collaborative research projects, feasibility studies, and technology transfer activities.

Process

How to Request a Service

  1. Contact the BRTC Coordinator (details in sidebar) with your requirement
  2. A faculty expert will review the request and provide a quotation
  3. Submit sample / project brief along with payment confirmation
  4. Testing / consultancy is conducted in the relevant department laboratory
  5. Official report is issued on department letterhead
