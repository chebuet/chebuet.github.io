How to Reach Us

🚌

By Bus

Take any bus to Palashi or Nilkhet stop on Dhaka's Mirpur Road. BUET main gate is a short walk from either stop.

🚆

By Metro Rail

Exit at Shahbagh Station on the MRT-6 line. From there BUET is approximately 700 m on foot or a short rickshaw ride.

🚗

By Car / Rideshare

Navigate to "BUET, Palashi, Dhaka" on Google Maps or Pathao. Visitor parking is available near the main gate.
